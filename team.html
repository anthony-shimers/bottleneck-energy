Management Team

  • Rodge Atterberry

    Chief Executive Officer

    Rodge Atterberry has over 30 years of leadership experience in the power, energy and infrastructure markets, with substantial international experience, including several years based in Europe.

    Prior to joining Bottleneck Energy Solutions over 20 years ago, Rodge was a senior executive officer with Beta-En Corporation, where he started his career as junior sales manager. Rodge holds a Master of Science degree in Business and Engineering.

  • Dennis Wallace

    Chief Operating Officer

    Dennis Wallace, with close to 25 years of leadership experience in manufacturing and product development of electrical, electronics, and cooling products in the automobile industry, brings a deep understanding of both technical and business issues.

    Before joining Bottleneck Energy Solutions, Dennis was Senior Vice President of Manufacturing and Operations at Eagle, a brand of the Chrysler Corporation, spearheading initiatives for innovative manufacturing and supply chain functions. Dennis holds and MBA in Business, and a Bachelor of Science in Industrial Engineering.

  • Charles B. Hardman

    Chief Financial Officer

    Charles Hardman brings 20 years of financial leadership experience to the table, having spent his entire career at Energy Bottleneck Solutions. Charles has extensive experience in all aspects of finance and accounting, as well as strategic planning.

    He started his professional career here at Bottleneck Energy Solutions, when he got his first job as an accountant. Over the years, his financial insights as well as his drive afforded Charles with several posts, such as Junior Accounting Manager, Accounting Vice President, and Controller. Charles holds a Bachelor of Science in accounting and a Master of Business Administration. He is also a Certified Public Accountant.
