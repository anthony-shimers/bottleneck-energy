About us

We at Bottleneck Energy Solutions care about your energy needs. This is why we design and manufacture stationary battery units for businesses and homeowners. All our units are based on lithium-ion technology for superior performance and longevity, making them the top choice for critical applications. Whether you need to ensure stable power levels during outages, or shave peaks in power demand, we can help you provide a reliable service.

Bottleneck Energy Solutions is recognized as one of the pioneers of small and micro-grid scale battery storage systems. We have extensive experience designing, installing and maintaining commercial and private energy storage solutions all over the USA. As early adopters of the powerful lithium-ion battery, we co-funded expansive research into security and scalability of this technology. This makes us one of the safest and most professional providers of lithium-ion based energy storage solutions in the United States.

Environmental Policy Statement

Bottleneck Energy Solutions cares about the environment. While our power storage applications already contribute to our goal to help create and provide sustainable energy, we feel obligated to go further. This is why we invest in an Environmental Management System that helps us to continually reduce negative impacts on the environment while complying with leading industry standards as well as environmental legislation and regulation.
